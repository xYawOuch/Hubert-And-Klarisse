Our Love Story

Once Upon a Time...

In the enchanting land of the Philippines, nestled within the walls of a prestigious academy known as UERM College of Medicine, fate began to weave a magical tale. It was 2013 when Princess Klarisse, a determined young woman with dreams as boundless as the ocean, set foot in the realm of medicine. At the same time, a charming and steadfast Prince Hubert embarked on his own journey in the very same school and class. Though they had spent their childhoods in the same faraway kingdom called the United States of America, their paths had never crossed -until now.

The First Spark

During the first week of medical school, outside the anatomy building, their eyes met for the first time. Surrounded by mutual friends, they exchanged smiles and laughter, unaware of the invisible strings tying their hearts together. Little did Princess Klarisse know, Prince Hubert had already admired her from afar, having discovered her picture in their batch’s Facebook group. And what the prince didn’t realize was that, at that very first encounter, the princess, too, had taken notice of him -curious about the enigmatic boy who shared a similar origin.

By the end of their first week, their connection grew in subtle, unspoken ways. They joined their friends at a local tavern, where their laughter filled the air, and sparks began to fly. Yet, for all its potential, the timing was not yet right.

Trials and Triumphs

Amidst the grueling challenges of medical school, the prince and princess found solace in their growing friendship. When Princess Klarisse faced her first Biochemistry quiz, overwhelmed and unsure, Prince Hubert stepped in as her knight in shining armor. With encouragement and support, he reminded her that even in the most daunting moments, she was never alone. Their bond deepened, yet romance remained elusive.

One fateful day, just before an exam, Princess Klarisse was so engrossed in reviewing her notes that she failed to notice a speeding jeepney heading her way as she crossed the street. Out of nowhere, Prince Hubert appeared, pulling her back to safety in the nick of time. Breathless and shaken, Princess Klarisse looked up at him as he steadied her. "You need to be more careful," he said, his concern written all over his face. It was a moment she would never forget -a reminder of how he always seemed to be there when she needed him most.

As the first year of school passed, both endured heartbreak in their separate lives, mending their hearts one piece at a time. By their second year, their paths began to intertwine more closely, brought together by shared experiences and a mutual circle of friends. Prince Hubert’s kindness and persistence became unshakable. Whether suggesting a dinner date, a study session, or a Robin Williams movie marathon (knowing her childhood admiration for the actor), he remained steadfast in his efforts to win the princess over.

Each time, Princess Klarisse gently declined. When friends asked her why, she would reply, “I know we’d get along really well, but I’m scared. He seems like the type to break my heart.” She guarded her heart fiercely, determined to avoid further heartbreak. Yet, Prince Hubert never wavered, his patience and quiet determination was a testament to his intentions.

The Boracay Adventure

In October 2014, their friend group planned a trip to the pristine shores of Boracay. During the first two days, Princess Klarisse quietly observed Prince Hubert, intrigued by the boy who had persistently asked her out on multiple occasions -36 times on Messenger alone, to be exact (though the exact number of phone text messages and in person invitations remain in disagreement). Beneath his confident demeanor, she glimpsed the kind and genuine man he truly was.

On the second night, under a starlit sky by the ocean’s edge, the two found themselves alone for the first time, talking freely. Dreams, favorite books, movies, and songs poured forth, creating a connection neither could deny. It was then that Princess Klarisse, her curiosity and trust overcoming her fears, finally relented.

"Fine, I’ll let you take me out on that date," she declared, "but just this once." That magical first date, filled with parasailing, helmet diving, jet skiing, and a romantic dinner, marked the beginning of a love story that would never be confined to Boracay.

A Love That Grew

From that day forward, their story unfolded like the most beautiful of fairy tales. They shared study dates, adventures across continents, and quiet moments that spoke volumes. By October 27, 2015, about a year after their magical Boracay date, Prince Hubert asked Princess Klarisse to be his official partner. From then on, their love knew no bounds.

Their relationship blossomed, surviving the challenges of medical school, long-distance separations, and the rigors of life. Together, they traveled the world, supported one another through triumphs and trials, and became each other’s best friend.

Happily Ever After

On November 12, 2023, under a sky illuminated by music and lights at a festival in Orlando, FL, Prince Hubert asked Princess Klarisse to join him in the next chapter of their fairy tale. With The Chainsmokers serenading them and stars witnessing their moment, he knelt on one knee and proposed to the princess under the Electric Daisy sky.

Now, on October 25, 2025, after over a decade of shared memories, they will say “I do” at Paraiso De Avedad in San Pablo City, Laguna, surrounded by loved ones. Their fairy tale continues, an enduring testament to love, patience, and the magic of destiny -a happily ever after without “The End.”

Kids Zone

We are excited to welcome your little ones to the celebration and want to ensure they have a magical experience of their own! Our wedding will feature a special Kids Zone, designed with fun and safety in mind. While the adults enjoy the cocktail hour and reception, the children will have their own dedicated area to play, eat, and create memories.

The Kids Zone will feature a charming treehouse and a playground, offering plenty of space for the children to explore and enjoy games and activities. There will be fun, age-appropriate games to keep them entertained throughout the event.

Children age 13 and under will also have their own seating and dining area, where they will enjoy their meals in a fun and safe environment. Please take note that the children will not be seated with their parents during the reception. Their food will be served at designated times, so they can eat alongside their friends in their own space.

To make sure the Kids Zone runs smoothly, we will be providing nannies to supervise the children, ensuring they are well taken care of. If you prefer to bring your own nanny, please let the bride and groom know in advance, and we'll make arrangements accordingly.

Each child will also have a kid-friendly menu tailored to their tastes. Please make sure to note any dietary restrictions and allergies when confirming your RSVP, so we can accommodate all needs and ensure the children have a safe and enjoyable meal.

We hope the kids enjoy their time at the Kids Zone, allowing the adults to relax and enjoy the festivities. Thank you for helping us create an unforgettable celebration for everyone!
